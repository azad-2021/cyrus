We are Cyrus

We have been operating since 1986 in the field of electronic security system in manufacturing, installing and servicing field. We are in security business with one prime motive and that is to provide Efficient, Effective and Economic solution for the need of financial institutions and other organizations.

  • We are the first to introduce the concept of CCTV as a deterrent device in the banks and are also the first company to install CCTV in any Indian bank which was SBI Main Branch, Gorakhpur, U.P 1996.
  • We are the first company to get its equipment approved by the Reserve Bank of India.
  • We are the first company to manufacture Autodialers which is a silent security device to prevent crimes.
